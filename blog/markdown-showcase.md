## 01_TEXT_STYLES
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

*   **Bold**: **Extreme Emphasis**
*   **Italic**: *Subtle Note*
*   **Combined**: ***Critical Importance***
*   **Monospaced**: <samp>System Output</samp>
*   **Underlined**: <ins>Inserted Material</ins>
*   **Strike-through**: ~~Legacy Protocol~~
*   **Boxed**: <table><tr><td>Isolated Data Node</td></tr></table>
*   **Subscript**: $H_2O$ or log<sub>2</sub>(x)
*   **Superscript**: $2^{10}$ or 10<sup>-3</sup>
*   **Small Text**: <small>Telemetric Footer Data</small>
*   **Text Color**: $\color{gray}{Inactive}$ // $\color{green}{Active}$ // $\color{red}{Critical}$
